How to Register with Glen Rock Community School

Follow these simple steps to register your child for our robotics classes

Step 1

Notes: Clicking on the Register link on the schedule page, should get you to the login page from Community School.

Step 2

Notes: After logging in, select "Glen Rock Community School" as your option to register from.
