## **Year 2020-2021 at Coleman**

During the 2020-2021 COVID year, Coleman Elementary School operated virtually. 

Grade 1 team selected the Primary Problem, Grade 3-5 picked Technical category and both teams went a fully virtual format. 

Special Thanks Coleman HSA Leads Lauren Suero, Harneet Arora  and volunteer  parent coaches Alexis Larkin, Shilpa Mehta, Marina Skurko, and Varun Mehta for running the virtual classrooms.

### **Team 1 - Virtual Odyssey (Technical Category)**

Team Rubys - Dahlia, bored with her small town, discovers a mysterious video game shop. She buys a VR game that transports her to a 2D world where she must collect four rubies to return home. A shape-shifting creature tricks her, stealing her rubies. With help from a talking tree named O, Dahlia battles the creature, reclaims the rubies, and escapes back home, gaining new appreciation for her town.
