## What is Odyssey of the Mind?

[Odyssey of the Mind](https://odysseyofthemind.com/) is a creative, problem-solving educational experience where students work together to solve predefined long-term problems.

OM encourages teamwork across age groups, critical thinking, innovation, and presentation skills through an internationally recognized program supported by sponsors such as NASA, IBM, and Arm & Hammer. Through a problem-solving mindset using locally available resources and materials at home, teams shape the next generation of engineers, programmers, and critical thinkers.

### Types of Problems

- **Technical Challenges** (e.g., building devices that perform tasks)
- **Classics/Theatrical Challenges** involving art, mythology, or historical recreations
- **Vehicle-Based Design** and operation tasks
- **Performance-Based** creative writing and acting problems
- **Primary Problems** specifically designed for K-2 graders, introducing younger children to creativity and performance in a simpler format

### What do Kids Learn through Odyssey?

- **Problem-Solving Skills**: Tackling real-world challenges through design thinking
- **Collaboration**: Working in teams to achieve common goals, working with different age groups
- **Creativity**: Designing unique solutions to complex problems
- **Technical Skills**: Programming, engineering, and mechanical design
- **Presentation & Communication**: Sharing ideas and explaining technical concepts
