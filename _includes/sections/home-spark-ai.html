Introducing Spark.AI

Spark.AI teaches us how Math makes AI work.

The robots us use sensors, motors, and code, but what makes a machine intelligent? Math. Spark.AI pulls back the curtain. Students learn how algorithms are constructed, what the math behind them actually does, and why a model makes the decisions it makes.

For grades 6-10, Spark.AI is a hands-on deep dive into the internal workings of AI - connecting the math students already know to the algorithms shaping the world around them.
