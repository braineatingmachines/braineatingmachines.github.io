Introducing Spark.AI

Robotics taught us to build. AI teaches us to think.

The robots your child builds with us use sensors, motors, and code to interact with the world. Artificial intelligence is what takes that further — it's how machines learn from data, recognize patterns, and make decisions on their own. Understanding those fundamentals doesn't just make students better at robotics. It gives them a clearer picture of the technology shaping everything around them.

Spark.AI is our new program for grades 6–10: a hands-on introduction to AI that connects directly to what students already know from building and programming robots.
