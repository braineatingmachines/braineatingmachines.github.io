Our After-School Curriculum: 3 Progressive Modules

Module 1: Robot Basics & Movement

5 sessions • Build & Program

  • Build LEGO SPIKE Prime robot
  • Block-based coding fundamentals
  • Motors and mechanical systems
  • Navigation challenges

Module 2: Distance & Color Sensors

5 sessions • Sensing & Logic

  • Ultrasonic distance sensors
  • Color recognition & line-following
  • Conditional logic & decisions
  • Autonomous navigation

Module 3: Force & Multi-Sensor

5 sessions • Integration

  • Force sensor integration
  • Multi-sensor programming
  • Advanced debugging skills
  • Final demonstration

Assessment: Each module ends with a knowledge quiz and team challenge where students apply learned concepts to solve new problems.
