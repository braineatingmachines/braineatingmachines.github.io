Explore Robotics & Engineering with LEGO® Spike Prime

Brain Eating Machines empowers 4th-8th graders to build, code, and develop robotics skills using LEGO® Spike Prime educational kits. Our curriculum prepares students for competitive robotics opportunities.

We conduct programs in Bergen County in Glen Rock, Ridgewood, Ho-Ho-Kus, Fair Lawn, Paramus and other neighboring towns.

Explore Programs Learn More

What Students Learn

Our robotics curriculum combines hands-on robot design, programming, and innovation projects. Students develop technical skills, creative problem-solving abilities, and collaborative teamwork through structured learning and competitive challenges.

Build & Design

Design, build, and program LEGO robots to complete missions on a themed playing field. Learn mechanical engineering and creative problem-solving.

Code & Program

Write programs using block-based coding to control your robot's sensors and motors. Develop computational thinking and logical reasoning skills.

Team & Compete

Work as a team to prepare for regional and state competitions. Build collaboration, communication, and presentation skills.
